Understanding the Maturity Levels

The maturity score (0-100) calculated for your system corresponds to one of five levels. Each level represents a progressively more sophisticated and proactive approach to managing legacy system security and operations. Higher levels indicate better risk management, more predictable outcomes, and greater alignment with business goals.
